About
Us
Thatsshowbusiness is an innovative and disruptive platform designed to capture real time data and match Creatives
with Discoverers. We share a common set of values to empower others to bring their creative talents to the forefront. These values play a central part in what is honest, good, beneficial, important and useful in our industry. It's about providing solutions to the inherent problems that create the stumbling blocks within our industry. Our goal is to support and nurture Creatives with their promotion, copyright, collaboration and recognition by raising their profile and breaking down the barrier of entry into this sector through the power of a single platform, intelligence led community. We work continuously with our users to create innovative solutions that meet the needs to their global problems.

Founded by a former performer, Thatsshowbusiness has taken those early years experiences and combined them with a deep understanding of disruptive technologies such as Blockchain, corporate funding models and Artificial Intelligence to create a platform that exceeds the limitations of existing platforms. With a deep understanding of
the effect of the current copyright and plagiarism of creative work that plagues the industry, the aim was always to
build something to meet those challenges and provide a nurturing space for all.
Why we are doing this
The creative industry faces three major problems: IP crime, a loss of profit and the impact of social media. IP crime
is a huge industry wide issue that is an industry of its own, stripping away the rights of performers worldwide. The

resultant loss of profit and jobs impacts the entire industry. Not only are people less likely to enter the creative sector, when they do they are met with hurdles that other industries do not face. Finally, the advent of social media

platforms that fail to protect content owners has exacerbated these issues with exponential growth in online content markets that indiscriminately steal copyrighted material with impunity.

Our platform is designed to deal with each of these issues as a whole. We envisage a "fair" multi
dimensional approach which incorporates content recognition, equitable remuneration, collaboration that can be tracked and

traced, transparency and trust on data, and a marketplace that fuels the relationship between the Creative and the consumer to maximise income generation.

Our

mission
